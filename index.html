Minimized Privacy Policy

This policy summarizes how Yolana's website (yolana.com/syolana.com) uses your data.

1. Introduction: We respect your privacy. This section covers data collected via mail subscriptions, Google authentication, and commenting.

2. Google Authentication: If you sign in using Google services, we access and store your basic Google profile name and email address provided by Google APIs to identify your comments. We do not access your password or other sensitive Google data.

3. Email Subscriptions: If you subscribe to the newsletter, we store your email address solely to send you my latest poetry and cinematic clips. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Commenting: When you leave a whisper (comment), we store the text, your author name (derived from your Google profile), and the timestamp.

5. Third-Party Services: We use Google services (Google services APIs) for authentication. We do not sell or share your data with other third parties.

6. Contact: If you have questions about your data, contact me via the social links provided above.
